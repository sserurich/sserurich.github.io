IoT For Air Quality Monitoring

Hands On Experience In Building Air Quality IoT Devices

About

The objective of the session is to provide participants with hands-on experience on building an air quality IoT device. The session will start with an introduction to air pollution, its effects, different types of pollutants and air quality sensing technologies. Participants will be able to prototype and deploy an IoT device that is capable of collecting air quality data and transmitting it in real-time to a cloud-based platform for analysis.
